Kenny Wormald: A Brief Intro

Kenneth “Kenny” Wormald is an American dancer, actor, and reality television personality. He gained considerable recognition in his portrayal of Ren McCormack in the 2011 adaptation of Footloose, a role which has remained one of his most notable. Wormald also featured regularly in the MTV reality television series named Dancelife in 2007.
