Barbara Harmon Obituary, Death – Barbara Harmon, a beloved resident of Dunlap, TN, passed away on the morning of November 16, 2023, at the age of 85, at the NHC Health Care Center. Known for her passion for cooking, baking, and love for bluegrass music, Barbara’s life was rich with familial connections and cherished hobbies. Born to Grady and Cleo Lockhart, Barbara was preceded in death by her brother, Buford Lockhart. She is survived by her devoted husband of 67 years, Auby Harmon, two sons, Michael (Cheryl) Harmon and Dale Harmon, all residents of Dunlap.

Her legacy continues through her grandchildren: Cody (Kayla), Kyle (Tricia), and Paige Harmon, and great-grandchildren: Ellan, Anna, and Savannah Harmon. Barbara’s familial ties extended further with three sisters: Murdena (Jerry) Hixson, Patsy (Kevin) Lockhart, Cindy Garner, and two brothers: Don Lockhart of Lookout Mountain and Norman Lockhart of Dunlap. Numerous nieces and nephews also mourn her passing.

Funeral services to honor Barbara’s life will be held on Sunday at 3:00 p.m. CST in the chapel of Standefer-Reed Funeral Home, located at 50 May Road, Dunlap, TN. Elder Lynn Shuler will officiate the service, and the burial will take place in Rankin Cemetery. Prior to the service, the family will receive friends from 12:00 to 3:00 p.m. on Sunday. Expressions of sympathy and condolences can be shared online at www.reedfamilyfh.com. The funeral arrangements are entrusted to Standefer-Reed Funeral Home, offering support to the grieving family during this difficult time. Barbara Harmon’s memory will be cherished by her family, friends, and the Dunlap community she was a part of for so many years.
