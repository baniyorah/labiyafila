Bryan Biggs Obituary, Death – Bryan E. Biggs, a vibrant soul of 47 years, unexpectedly departed from our midst on Sunday, December 17, 2023, in Frankfort, Kentucky. Born on June 6, 1976, in Lexington, Kentucky, he was the beloved son of Eugene Biggs and Judy Shields Fuller. Bryan’s zest for life was evident in his diverse pursuits. As a general laborer, he embraced each day with a love for music, a passion for dance, and an unwavering commitment to creating good times. Bryan will be fondly remembered for his boundless generosity and, above all, his deep affection for his family.

Survived by his parents, Eugene Biggs and Judy Shields Fuller, Bryan leaves a lasting legacy in the hearts of those who knew and loved him. His three daughters, Cressa Fryrear, Marissa Smith of Moreland, and Hailey Biggs of Berea, Kentucky, were the recipients of his boundless love. Bryan also leaves behind his cherished granddaughter, Ember Caddell. In the extended embrace of family, he is remembered by his step-father, Gene Fuller of Moreland, his sister Melissa Hackney (Ray) of Nicholasville, Kentucky, and his brother Brandon Biggs of Moreland. Numerous other relatives and friends join in mourning the sudden departure of this remarkable individual.

A Public Graveside Service to celebrate Bryan’s life will be held at 1:00 PM on Thursday, December 21, 2023, at Moreland Cemetery. Guided by Bro. Gary Yocom, the service will be a heartfelt tribute to the memories shared with Bryan. Following the service, Bryan will find his final resting place in the peaceful embrace of Moreland Cemetery. To honor Bryan’s memory and support his family during this difficult time, memorial contributions are graciously accepted at Fox & Friend Funeral Home, c/o Bryan Biggs Memorial Fund, 312 West Main Street, Stanford, KY 40484. In commemorating Bryan’s life, let us find solace in the memories he gifted us and the love he so freely shared with those around him.
