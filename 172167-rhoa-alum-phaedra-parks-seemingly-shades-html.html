RHOA Alum Phaedra Parks Seemingly Shades RHOP Star Gizelle Bryant after Her WWHL Appearance

September 06, 2024
post
Feuds aren't a new thing amongst the women of the "Real Housewives" family, but this particular feud might just be crossing some franchise borders.

There seems to be a "The Boy Is Mine"-type situation involving former "Real Housewives of Atlanta" star Phaedra Parks, "Real Housewives of Potomac" star Gizelle Bryant and a certain "Mr. Chocolate."

Trouble started when reports claiming that Phaedra had cheated on her ex-husband Apollo Nida with a "Mr. Chocolate," surfaced online.

These reports gained more popularity when Apollo stated that the man in question was no other person than Gizelle's ex-husband, Pastor Jamal Bryant.

That piece of information isn't one that Gizelle is letting fly by as she addressed the issue during an episode of "Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen."

The reality star said although she had heard the rumors about her ex and Phaedra, she wasn't bothered by them.

Gizelle added that even if she finds that Phaedra and Jamal did have a thing, she wouldn't care as Phaedra is "unimportant."

Phaedra, who has always denied any romantic involvement with "Mr. Chocolate," took a different route to address the rumors. Taking to Instagram the 45-year-old posted a hot photo of herself with the caption:

"Even if it wasn't me.... it still wouldn't be you."

The caption was so shady that The Shade Room shared a screenshot of Phaedra's post with the caption:

"Oop!! #PhaedraParks seemingly responds to #GizelleBryant after she says she's unbothered by the rumours of Phaedra dating her ex-husband #JamalBryant."

Speculation that Gizelle and Jamal were back together started when photos of them together were shared to Gizelle's Instagram.

However, BravoTV confirmed that the couple was dating during the first part of the "Real Housewives of Potomac" Season 4 reunion aired Sunday night.

The report claims that Gizelle previously denied reconciliation rumors during her appearance on WWHL back in May. The 49-year-old had stated:

"We're very close. We have a great relationship. We're great. But we're not officially back together or anything like that. But we are great friends."
