Kevin Federline is an American singer along with being a former backup dancer. He is known for his child custody battle and his two-year marriage to Britney Spears.

He made his album debut by releasing Playing with Fire in 2006. He has given his appearances in many films and tv series like The Drew Carey Show, Nikki, You Got Served, WWE Raw, The Onion Movie, One Tree Hill, and many more.

Kevin Federline Wife

Kevin Federline was married to Britney Spears on 18 September 2004 at a residence in Studio City, California. They got engaged in July 2004.

Britney filed for divorce on 7 November 2006 from Kevin and their divorce was finalized on 30 July 2007. Then he was married to former volleyball player Victoria Prince on 10 August 2013.

He had been in a relationship with actress Shar Jackson and got engaged also with her but later they separated. 

Is Kevin Federline Still Married?

Yes, Kevin Federline is still married to Victoria Prince. This couple got married in 2013 and have been living happily since their marriage.

Kevin Federline Children

Kevin Federline has a daughter Kori Madison and a son Kaleb Michael Jackson Federline from his ex-girlfriend actress Shar Jackson.

He has two sons Sean Preston Federline and Jayden James Federline from his ex-wife Britney Spears. He has two daughters named Peyton Marie Federline and Jordan Kay Federline from his current wife Victoria.

Kevin Federline Age

Kevin Federline is 44 years old as of 2022. He was born as Kevin Earl Federline on 21 March 1978 to Mike Federline and Julie Bleak.
