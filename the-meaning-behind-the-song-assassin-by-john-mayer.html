At first glance, “Assassin” seems like a typical love song, but upon closer inspection, the meaning behind the lyrics becomes clearer. The song tells the story of a man who is deeply in love with someone who doesn’t reciprocate his affection. Despite this, he still spends time with her, secretly hoping that maybe one day, she’ll fall in love with him too.

As the song progresses, it becomes evident that the man is aware of the fact that the woman he’s in love with is never going to love him back. Despite this, he’s convinced that she’ll come around and fall in love with him eventually. In the end, however, the man realizes that his love is one-sided and that he needs to move on. The lyrics of “Assassin” are incredibly relatable and comforting, especially for those who have ever loved someone and not received love in return.
