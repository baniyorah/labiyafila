Boys Planet Contestants That Had Previously Participated in the LOUD Competition

September 01, 2024
post

In 2021, “LOUD,” a boy band reality competition show, debuted on SBS. Together, J.Y. Park’s JYP Entertainment and PSY’s P Nation created two international K-pop boy groups for the show. On May 17, 2022, P Nation’s final six contestants made their debut as TNX.

As part of Mnet’s ongoing effort to create a global K-pop boy group, “Boys Planet” pits 93 male trainees against one another in a reality survival competition. On “Boys Planet,” several of the “LOUD” survival contestants are now competing. Let’s look at them now.

Lee Seung Hwan

South Korean singer and dancer Lee Seung Hwan is presently taking part in the “Boys Planet” competition. Before, he was a member of the project group 1THE9, which was established as a result of the reality survival program “Under Nineteen.” He took part in the “LOUD” boy band survival audition program in 2021, but he was eliminated during the first round of the competition.

Lee Ye Dam

Lee Ye Dam is a South Korean singer and dancer who performs for LM Entertainment. This K-Group candidate from “Boys Planet” also appeared on “LOUD” in 2021. During the eleventh episode of the show, he received a casting offer from both JYP Entertainment and P Nation, but he decided to accept the offer from P Nation. In the seventh round, he ultimately lost.

Kim Min Seoung

Kim Min Seoung is a South Korean singer and dancer who performs under the name Redstart ENM. Min Seoung was revealed to be a “LOUD” competitor on May 20, 2021. He was eliminated from the tournament in the fifth round. In December of last year, he appeared as a contestant on “Boys Planet” on Mnet.

Park Han Bin

South Korean singer and dancer Park Han Bin performs under the WAKEONE label. He participated in “LOUD” in 2021. During the first round of the competition, he was eliminated. Han Bin is a K-Group contestant competing on Mnet’s ongoing reality survival show “Boys Planet” right now.

Haruto

One of the international candidates on “Boys Planet” is Maeda Haruto. He performs under the WAKEONE label and is a Japanese singer and dancer. He previously participated in “LOUD,” the boy band reality survival audition competition from JYP Entertainment and P Nation. Unfortunately, Haruto was ejected from the competition in the fourth round.
