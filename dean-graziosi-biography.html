Who is Dean Graziosi?

Dean Graziosi is an American real estate investor, author, philanthropist, and leading trainer.

He is well known for his interview-type TV shows and for teaching about a successful mindset.

Birth Facts, Early Life

Dean Graziosi was born on 20 November 1968 in Marlboro New York, United States of America. As of now, he is 53 years old. He was born to Paul Graziosi and Gloria Post. He is the youngest child among the two of them. His parents separated when he was a toddler.

Therefore, he grew up with his mother and grandmother until 13 years old.

Later, he went along with his father. In an interview, he said that he lived in a bathroom with his father for a year.

He holds citizenship in the USA and his ethnic background is caucasian.

Likewise, his astrological sign is Scorpio. Regarding his education, Graziosi matriculated in 1986 from Marlboro High School.

Career, Professional Life

He started his career as a businessman when he was 20 years old. He at first bought, fixed, and sold old or trashed cars. after some experiences, he created a training course for real estate “Think A Little Different” in 2002.

In 2006, he wrote his first book “Totally Fulfilled”, published by Vanguard Press.

It took its place on New York Times best-seller list.

He then published other books such as Profit From Real Estate Right Now!, Millionaire Success Habits, The Better Life Journal 4PK, The Better Life Journal, and Better Life Journal – 4 Pack.

Dean has appeared on various shows and podcasts such as Tony Robbins: I Am Not Your Guru, Connected: The Joe Polish Stor, WeRiseUP, Evolvepreneur Podcast Show, and The Best You Expo, Growth Now Movement with Justin Schenck, and NotBasicBlonde Podcast.

Moreover, he is also the co-founder of The Centurion Mastermind Group which has donated over $1 million to the charity organization, Virgin Unite.

In the year 2007, he founded “Operation Free Home”, which assists homeless people with accommodation.
