Who is Marilyn Manson?

Brian Hugh Warner, commonly known as Marilyn Manson is an American singer, former music journalist, composer, actor, painter, songwriter, and author.

He is the co-founder and lead singer of the band Marilyn Manson. Additionally, he has also appeared in numerous movies and television series including ‘Lost Highway’ and ‘Bowling for Columbine’ among others.

Marilyn Manson: Early Life, Childhood, and Education

Manson was born in Canton, Ohio on January 5, 1969, to parents Barbara Warner Wyer and Hugh Angus Warner. In his childhood years, he attended his mother’s Episcopal church.

He became interested in the world of music in his childhood years. He is of American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to a mixed ethnic background of German, English, and Irish.

Talking about his education, Marilyn Manson attended Heritage Christian School. He later transferred to GlenOak High School. Additionally, he also attended Broward Community College in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Marilyn Manson: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Manson initially formed Marilyn Manson & the Spooky Kids alongside guitarist Scott “Daisy Berkowitz” Putesky. The band’s name was later shortened.

Additionally, the band earned a contract from Trent Reznor’s record label, Nothing. In addition, they also earned them a spot on Nine Inch Nails’ 1994 tour.

Their first album was titled ‘Portrait of an American Family. They also released their second album, ‘Antichrist Superstar’, and the album debuted at No. 3 on the Billboard 200 chart.

Apart from his musical career, Manson has also released his autobiography with rock journalist Neil Strauss, titled ‘The Long Road Out of Hell’.

Manson has also appeared in several movies and television series including ‘Salem’, ‘Let Me Make You a Martyr’, ‘Street Level’, ‘Sons of Anarchy, ‘Once Upon a Time, ‘Eastbound & Down, ‘Californication’, ‘Wrong Cops’, ‘Number One!’, ‘Tim and Eric Awesome Show, Great Job!’, and ‘Rise: Blood Hunter’ among others.

Additionally, Marilyn Manson also has 23 credits as a composer, 8 credits as a director, and 7 credits as a producer. In addition, he has also provided soundtracks for over 100 movies and television series.

His other albums include ‘Antichrist Superstar’, ‘Holy Wood’, ‘Eat Me, Drink Me, ‘Born Villain’, and ‘The Pale Emperor’ among others. Furthermore, Manson also supported various charitable causes.

Manson has garnered MTV Video Music Award nominations two times. Additionally, Manson has also received four Grammy Award nominations. Furthermore, he also received Kerrang Awards’ Lifetime achievement award in 2015.

Marilyn Manson has not revealed his current salary. However, he has an estimated net worth of around $25 million at present.
