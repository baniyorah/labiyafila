Jersey Poffs Obituary to held at Wayne Street Church, St. Marys, Ohio

post

Saint Marys, Ohio – The Saint Marys community is reeling from the heartbreaking loss of 11-year-old Jersey June Poff, who passed away on September 3, 2023, at Dayton Children’s Hospital.

Jersey’s family and friends are preparing to bid their final farewells at a funeral service scheduled for 10:00 AM on September 8, 2023, at the Wayne Street Church in Saint Marys. Following the service, Jersey will find her eternal rest at Elm Grove Cemetery.

Jersey June Poff was a bright presence at Saint Marys Intermediate School, and her absence will be deeply felt by both her fellow students and teachers.

In memory of Jersey, her family has suggested that donations be made to the Saint Marys All Abilities Park Fund. This heartfelt gesture aims to honor Jersey’s memory by contributing to a cause close to her heart.

The Saint Marys community joins Jersey’s family in mourning the loss of this young soul and extends their support during this difficult time.

With heavy hearts and profound sadness, we come together to remember the beautiful life of Jersey Poff, a cherished member of the Ohio community, whose time with us was far too short.

Jersey, a vibrant and spirited 5th-grade student at St. Mary’s schools, was a young soul who radiated an infectious passion for life. Her exuberance was a constant presence, whether she was sharing her radiant smile, filling the air with hearty laughter, singing with boundless joy, dancing with abandon, or tackling challenges with unwavering determination.

As the St. Marys community and schools grapple with the heartbreaking loss of Jersey, our thoughts and prayers are with her family, friends, and all who were deeply touched by her incredible life.

This week undoubtedly brings a heavy burden to the Roughrider community, and the school is providing additional support to help students and staff cope with this grievous loss.

Jersey Poff, the beloved daughter of Alison June Poff and Jody Poff, was a shining light within the St. Marys community. Her bright and infectious spirit left an indelible mark on the lives of all who had the privilege of knowing her.
