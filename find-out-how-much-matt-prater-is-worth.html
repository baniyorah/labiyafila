Get to Know Matt Prater

Matthew Phillip Prater (born August 10, 1984) is a successful American football placekicker who currently plays for the Detroit Lions of the National Football League (NFL). He first joined the Lions as an undrafted free agent in 2006 and developed his skills playing college football at Central Florida. Notably, Prater has solidified his place in NFL history by holding the record for the longest field goal at 64 yards. This remarkable feat was achieved on December 8, 2013, in Denver during a game against the Tennessee Titans. After completing a suspension for violating the NFL’s substance abuse policy, Prater was released by the Denver Broncos.
