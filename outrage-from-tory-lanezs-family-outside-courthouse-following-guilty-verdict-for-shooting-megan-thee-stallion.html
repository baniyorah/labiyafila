Outrage from Tory Lanezs Family Outside Courthouse Following Guilty Verdict for Shooting Megan Th

August 22, 2024
post

Tory Lanez’s father, Sonestar Peterson, criticized the police, prosecutors, and rapper Jay-Z and the Roc Nation, to which Megan Thee Stallion is signed, following his son’s guilty verdict for shooting the female rapper.

The Canadian singer’s father passionately voiced his concerns about the justice system outside the courthouse as his son was taken into custody after the guilty ruling.

Expressing his frustration, he stated, “I just stood here in this Los Angeles and witnessed the worst miscarriage of justice that this world has ever seen. You want to know how I feel, I’ll tell you exactly how I feel.”

 

He then singled out Megan’s lawyer, Alex Spiro, and Desiree Perez, the COO of Roc Nation, saying; “And the whole wicked system of Roc Nation, including you Jay-Z.”

 

Peterson also alleged that certain witnesses were collaborating with prosecutors and added, “I’ve seen so much evidence buried in this. I know exactly what the public will say.” 

 

He also appealed to “all the young brothers to rise up … because your sons have been done dirty.” He concluded his impassioned speech by saying; 

 

“This court system is not for justice. It’s about wins and losses.

“Jay-Z, let me speak to you. Because my son refused to sign a Roc Nation deal with you. And Desiree Perez you wicked witch, you snitch.

“We don’t shoot you with guns, but we know exactly how to bring you down in spirit.” 

Tory’s stepmother also voiced her anger over the case, emphasizing that it is far from over. 

The singer is facing a potential 22-year prison sentence and deportation. Tory’s sentencing is scheduled for January 27.
