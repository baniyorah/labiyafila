Tamera Mowry gets slammed yet again for saying her husband is not a racist

September 09, 2024
post

Tamera Mowry-Housley went on full defensive mode trying to protect her husband from critics calling him a racist.

Having a job as a journalist is not easy, especially since anything you say can be taken out of context. After several years of being married to Tamera Mowry, a proud black woman, Adam Housley still gets criticized for apparently being racist.

Recently, he shared a photo of his family, which included his husband Tamera and their two children, people were quick to notice how his caption spoke about craziness on "all sides".

Users thought that this could actually be a racist comment, especially since President Donald Trump recently used the statement "some very fine people on both sides", using it to describe white and colored people.

With Housley's former connection to Fox News and his own race, people were quick to jump into their own conclusions of him using the same statement to describe his own family.

However, having enough of the rumors, Tamera made sure to defend him on her own daytime show "The Real".

“There are some people who think that my husband is racist because he worked for a certain channel. Ima look in that camera right there and let everybody know, my husband is not a racist.”

In fact, Adam is such a loving husband and father, often sharing precious moments with his family both at home and while they are out touring the world together. Despite everything that life throws at him, and despite how tired he gets at times, he always makes sure to keep a smile on his face and that's because of how much he loves his family. After all, his interracial family is one of the clearest reasons as to why he is definitely not a racist.
